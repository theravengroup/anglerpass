The Opportunity

A Premier Ecosystem, Running on Manual Systems

Private fly fishing has an access problem. Not a lack of interest — a lack of infrastructure.

Scattered Management

Landowners manage private water access through phone calls, emails, and handshake agreements. There's no centralized system to organize properties, schedules, or inquiries.

Club Friction

Fly fishing clubs handle memberships, scheduling, and communications through spreadsheets and group texts. Administrative overhead eats into the time members should spend on the water.

Discovery Gaps

Individual anglers have limited ways to find and book legitimate private water experiences. Most access still depends on who you know, not what's available.

Trust Deficit

Without a structured platform, trust between landowners, clubs, and anglers relies on informal networks. That limits growth and keeps the market fragmented.

The AnglerPass Philosophy

The best fishing isn't about finding any water. It's about finding the right water — and earning access to it.

Platform Capabilities

Core Platform Features

Every tool designed to make private water management, membership operations, and angler access work together.

Property Registration

List and manage private waters with detailed profiles, maps, and access parameters. Define what's available, when, and to whom.

Membership Tools

Run club memberships with structured tiers, renewals, and digital roster management. Keep your member base organized without the spreadsheets.

Reservations & Booking

A clean workflow for scheduling fishing days. Handle requests, confirmations, and calendar management from a single dashboard.

Availability Calendars

Visual, real-time calendars that sync across properties and members. No more double bookings or outdated schedules.

Access Controls

Fine-grained permissions for who can see, request, and book specific waters. Maintain privacy while opening controlled access.

Guest Management

Handle member guests, day-pass visitors, and inquiry forms. Track who's on the water and when, with clear records for every visit.

Admin Dashboard

A centralized operations view for landowners and club managers. See activity, manage requests, and monitor access from one screen.

Inquiry Handling

Receive and respond to access requests from qualified anglers. Pre-screen, approve, or decline with structured communication tools.

Private Water Listings

Curated, searchable listings for private waters. Each property gets a professional profile with photos, rules, species info, and access terms.

The Business Model

SaaS Subscriptions

Tiered monthly plans for landowners and clubs based on properties managed, members served, and feature access. Predictable, recurring revenue from day one.

Booking Transaction Fees

A percentage on private water bookings processed through the platform. As the marketplace grows, transaction revenue scales alongside subscription revenue.

Premium Access Tiers

Individual anglers pay for enhanced discovery, priority booking, and access to exclusive or high-demand properties — a consumer revenue layer on top of B2B SaaS.
